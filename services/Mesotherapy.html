    What is Mesotherapy?

    Mesotherapy is a minimally invasive cosmetic procedure that involves injecting small amounts of various substances directly into the mesoderm, the middle layer of the skin. It was pioneered in the 1950s by French physician Dr. Michel Pistor and has since gained popularity worldwide as a versatile treatment for various aesthetic and medical conditions. The technique behind mesotherapy revolves around the concept of delivering active ingredients directly to the target area, bypassing the digestive system and allowing for more concentrated and effective results. The substances injected during mesotherapy can include vitamins, minerals, amino acids, enzymes, plant extracts, and medications, depending on the specific needs and goals of the individual undergoing treatment.

    Since mesotherapy is controlled straightforwardly to the ideal zone, it is accepted that side effects are restricted or decreased. Revealed symptoms of mesotherapy include:

    • Skin diseases
    • Immediate or postponed allergic response to the infused drugs/arrangements
    • Pigmentation at the infusion site
    • Lecithin is known to irritate and expand
    • Bruising at the infusion site
    • Ulceration, scarring and disfigurement at the infusion site
    • Panniculitis.

    What are the advantages of mesotherapy?

    • Your Skin Gets A High Concentrate Of Vitamins And Other Ingredients mesotherapy formulations can be customized to suit the unique needs and goals of each individual. Depending on the specific condition being treated, various substances such as vitamins, minerals, amino acids, enzymes, and medications can be combined in different concentrations to achieve optimal results.
    • Gradual, Natural-Looking Results It typically produces gradual results that develop over time, allowing for a more natural-looking outcome. This gradual improvement is often preferred by individuals who want subtle enhancements without drastic changes to their appearance.
    • It Can Be An Alternative To Expensive Surgeries It very well may be a portion of your anti-maturing skin routine and is less obtrusive than different techniques and plastic surgery procedures. You don't have to go under the blade to look better. The concentrated serums are conveyed to your skin utilizing needles, and you get normal-looking, sound skin. Likewise, there is no downtime.
    • Minimally Invasive Unlike surgical procedures, mesotherapy is minimally invasive, involving only small injections into the mesoderm layer of the skin. This means less trauma to the surrounding tissues, minimal discomfort during the procedure, and typically shorter recovery times compared to surgery.
    • Targeted Treatment Mesotherapy allows for precise targeting of specific areas, whether it's to rejuvenate the skin, reduce cellulite, contour the body, or promote hair growth. By delivering active ingredients directly to the desired area, mesotherapy ensures that the treatment is focused on the problem at hand, maximizing effectiveness.
    • It Keeps Your Skin Hydrated Mesotherapy can likewise help boost the skin's dampness substance and cause it to show up stout, firm, and sound.
